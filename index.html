What Is Udacity For College

Udacity for College is a peer-to-peer community of learners. A college-centric community where the members learn through various technical sessions, hosted by fellow college-mates

Why Udacity for College?

Teaching is best form of learning. Moreover, it is amazing to be learn new things in a friendly environment where, the teacher is your friend. The aim is to inculcate a peer-to-peer and self-learning environment so that you can learn more than just what is taught in the class

How does it work?

Udacity for College is headed by a community oriented Udacity Nanodegree student. The college ambassadors of their specific chapters host various events around their domain of Nanodegree. These events can be attended by any college student who is interested in enhancing their proficiency and knowledge in different computer science areas

Get involved!

  • Make sure there is not a Udacity for College chapter in your college already! If yes, go ahead join them and grow the community.
  • Please also read the Community Guidelines and FAQs before the next step.
  • Fill out the Udacity for College, Ambassador starter form.
  • Once submitted, we’ll reach out to you soon to discuss the further steps.
